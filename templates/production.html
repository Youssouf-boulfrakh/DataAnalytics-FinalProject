Concrete Price vs. Production

Price continues to rise but production has leveled off 7 years ago. Demand does not appear higher than supply. What else could be driving up the cost? Coal is a major ingredient needed for cement plants. Transportation of the ingredients such as sand. Transportation of the final product could be another factor. A multiple regression analysis might be considered.
